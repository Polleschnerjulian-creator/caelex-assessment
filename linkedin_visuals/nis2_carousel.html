Article 21: 10 Mandatory Measures
Every essential entity must implement all of these.
a
Risk analysis and information system security policies
b
Incident handling and response procedures
c
Business continuity and crisis management
d
Supply chain security assessment
e
Security in network and information systems acquisition
f
Vulnerability handling and disclosure
g
Cybersecurity risk assessment effectiveness
h
Basic cyber hygiene and training
i
Cryptography and encryption policies
j
Human resources security and access control

Article 23: Incident Reporting
Strict timelines. No exceptions.
Within 24 Hours
Early warning to national CSIRT or competent authority.
Initial assessment of the incident.
Within 72 Hours
Full incident notification including severity assessment,
impact analysis, and indicators of compromise.
Within 1 Month
Final report with root cause analysis,
mitigation measures taken, and cross-border impact.
NIS2 Directive, Art. 23
