Core Compliance Modules
15 specialized modules covering the full space regulatory lifecycle

Tier 1: Essential Modules Essentials+

The foundational 8 modules covering EU Space Act core obligations, available on all paid plans.

Authorization & Licensing
Art. 5-9, 32-39, 105-108
Multi-NCA workflow management with pathway selection (EUSPA registration, national authorization, Commission decision). Timeline tracking, document preparation, and NCA correspondence logging.
Registration & URSO
Art. 24 (Union Register of Space Objects)
Space object data validation and submission management for the EU Union Register. Status tracking, automated data quality checks, and registration lifecycle management.
Cybersecurity & NIS2
Art. 74-95, NIS2 Art. 21-23
Risk analysis framework with ENISA controls mapping (3.1.1-3.1.2). ISO 27001 and NIST CSF alignment, incident response procedures, and 72-hour reporting workflows.
Debris Mitigation & Safety
Art. 10-12, 58-72, 101-103
UN COPUOS and IADC guidelines compliance. End-of-life planning (25-year deorbit rule), collision avoidance procedures, passivation tracking, and disposal verification.
Environmental Compliance
Art. 96-100
Environmental Footprint Declaration management. Launch site assessment, protected area compliance, lifecycle impact analysis, and REACH regulation mapping.
Insurance & Liability
Art. 44-51
Third-party liability requirements by jurisdiction. Coverage minimums tracking, financial guarantee management, insurance binding workflows, and claims documentation.
Supervision & Reporting
Art. 26-31, 40-43, 52-57, 73
Ongoing supervisory obligations with automated incident reporting. Regular compliance reporting, NCA inspection readiness, and deadline management.
Regulatory Intelligence
Art. 104, 114-119
AI-powered monitoring of delegated and implementing acts. National law change tracking, regulatory feed with alerts, and impact assessment on existing compliance.

Compliance intelligence embedded at every workflow touchpoint

Architecture

  • 22+ specialized compliance tools

Assure: Investor Readiness Platform
Transforming regulatory compliance into a fundraising asset

Assure is a dedicated SaaS module that helps space startups present their compliance posture to investors, turning regulatory readiness into a competitive advantage during fundraising.

Exhibit 8 Assure Platform: Core Capabilities
Investment Readiness Score (IRS)
Multi-component scoring across regulatory, financial, technical, and market dimensions. Weighted composite score with trend tracking.
Regulatory Readiness Score (RRS)
Deep compliance assessment (0-100) measuring coverage across all applicable regulations. Daily automated recalculation.
Regulatory Credit Rating (RCR)
Enterprise-grade credit-style rating (A-F) for compliance maturity. Benchmarked against peer companies at similar stage.
Company Profile Builder
8-section comprehensive profile: leadership, financials, technology, market, team, traction, regulatory, and risk assessment.
Encrypted Data Rooms
AES-256-GCM encrypted document sharing. 4 access levels (View, Comment, Contribute, Full). Time-limited access with watermarking.
Cryptographic Attestations
SHA-256 hash-chained, tamper-evident compliance attestations. Verifiable by third parties without account creation.
Peer Benchmarking
Anonymous comparison against companies at similar stage, jurisdiction, and operator type. Percentile ranking across compliance dimensions.
Investor Network
Cap table management, investor milestone tracking, update distribution, and secure communication channels.
Strategic Value

Assure creates a unique flywheel: startups use Caelex for compliance, Assure generates investor-facing attestations that validate their regulatory maturity, and investors gain standardized compliance due diligence — reducing fundraising friction across the entire space startup ecosystem.

Strategic Thesis

Phase 1: Tool → Platform

Caelex has already crossed the threshold from single-purpose compliance tool to multi-module platform with assessment engines, AI copilot, investor readiness, and stakeholder collaboration. The 161-model database and 400+ API routes reflect genuine platform depth.

Phase 2: Platform → Infrastructure

The next evolution is becoming the regulatory infrastructure layer for European space: the system-of-record that NCAs, insurers, investors, and operators all rely on. Cryptographic attestations, data rooms, and the stakeholder network are early building blocks of this vision.

Conclusion

Caelex occupies a unique position as the only full-stack space compliance platform in Europe. With mandatory enforcement approaching in 2030 and 2,000+ companies needing compliance infrastructure, the platform is well-positioned to become the regulatory operating system for the European space industry — replacing fragmented consulting engagements with a unified, AI-powered digital compliance layer.
